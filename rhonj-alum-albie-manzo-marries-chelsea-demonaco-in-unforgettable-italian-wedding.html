RHONJ alum Albie Manzo marries Chelsea DeMonaco in unforgettable Italian wedding

Albie Manzo is off the market.

The “Real Housewives of New Jersey” alum, 37, tied the knot with Chelsea DeMonaco in a lavish Italian wedding ceremony Wednesday.

The couple confirmed the happy news by reposting their friends and family’s images from the big day, in which the bride wore a stunning lace gown, on social media.

Her off-the-shoulder dress featured a structured, corset bodice with bishop sleeves, a long train and a dramatic, sheer veil.

DeMonaco, 29, styled her brunette tresses in loose waves, while the groom looked dapper in a tux, paired with a multicolored boutonniere.

“Home for the next week 🤍👰🏽‍♀️🤵🏽‍♂️🇮🇹,” DeMonaco captioned a photo of the Italian countryside ahead of their nuptials.

Meanwhile, the mother of the groom, Caroline Manzo, took to social media hours before the couple said “I do.”

“Happy wedding day … We love you!” the reality star captioned an Instagram Story.

Caroline’s eldest son announced his engagement to his longtime partner back in April.

.

He surprised his bride-to-be by claiming they were going to a birthday dinner for his dad.

However, the surprise bash was actually organized to celebrate their engagement.

“She said YES!!!! Congratulations to @albiemanzo and @chelseademonaco on their engagement!” Caroline, 62, gushed at the time.

“We love you both to bits and are excited for this new chapter in all of our lives. Wedding bells will be ringing!!!!” the Bravolebrity added.

DeMonaco, for her part, called the evening “unforgettable” in her own post.

“Also big shout out to Big Al for taking the hit on what was supposed to be his birthday dinner celebration. Promise we will gift you with some gran babies eventually 🤍,” she added, referencing Albie’s dad, Albert “Big Al” Manzo.

The couple started dating in 2019 and confirmed their romance with a sweet vacation snap taken in New York City during the holidays.
