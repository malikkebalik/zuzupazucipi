Pregnant Rihanna honored with a statue at Met Gala 2022


The Met Gala 2022 paid a special tribute to pregnant Rihanna by creating a marble statue in her honor. The singer, who is expecting her first child with rapper-boyfriend A$AP Rocky, was unable to attend the event but still managed to captivate the audience.

Rihanna expressed her gratitude on Instagram, sharing a video of the statue showcased at the Metropolitan Museum of Art. She wrote, “Shut down the Met in marble! What’s more gilded than that? Lol! Thank you for this historic tribute! Y’all bad for this one.”

The museum’s gesture was well-received by Rihanna, who appreciated the honor. The post also included a video related to the subject.
