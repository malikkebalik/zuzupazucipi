Uzo Aduba Wiki – Bio: Age, Height, Parents, Family

Uzoamaka Nwanneka Aduba was born in the year 1981 in Boston, Massachusetts, the United States. She was born to Nigerian parents who are from the Igbo origin. She celebrates her birthday on 10th February, which makes her age 37.

Aduba grew up in Medfield, Massachusetts, along with her younger brother, Obi. Her brother was a former hockey player at the University of Massachusetts. She belongs to Nigerian descent and possesses a height of 5ft 6ins.

Aduba completed her high school education from the Medfield High Schoo. She later joined the College of Fine Arts at Boston University where she studied classical voice. Later, Aduba chose acting in replacement of the vocal career. Her acting teacher had encouraged her to pursue an acting career.
