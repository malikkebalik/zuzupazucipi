Who is Dillon Danis?

Dillon Danis is an American mixed martial artist. Currently, Dillon Danis is signed with Bellator MMA and is known for his amazing Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu techniques.

He won the IBJJF Boca Raton International Open Champion for black belts in 2015.

Dillon Danis: Career, Salary, Net Worth

Danis previously used to street fight at his school. After spending time with his coach Jason Lodato, he soon developed skills in fighting.

He went through the jiu-jitsu belt system at Jamie Cruz’s academy. Additionally, he also joined Marcelo Garcia’s academy in New York.

After turning 19, Dais decided to train full-time at Marcelo Garcia’s academy. Eventually, he earned his black belt on April 13, 2015.

His main achievements in Jiu-Jitsu include being IBJJF Pan American No-Gi Champion, IBJJF New York Spring Open Champion, and IBJJF Boca Raton International Open Champion.

Danis achieved further success after he joined the popular Irish mixed martial arts (MMA) fighter Conor McGregor in 2016 as a coach and main grappling training partner.

Furthermore, he also made his MMA debut at Bellator 198 against Kyle Walker and won the fight by submission.

Danis has not revealed his current salary. Additionally, he has an estimated net worth of $3 million.
