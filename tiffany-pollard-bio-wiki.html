Tiffany Pollard Bio (Wiki)

Who is Tiffany Pollard?

Tiffany Pollard is an American actress and American reality television personality. She appeared in the VH1’s ‘Flavor of Love and ‘I Love New York.

Additionally, she also took part in the British television series ‘Celebrity Big Brother’ and finished in fourth place.

Who are the parents of Tiffany Pollard?

Pollard was born in Utica, New York on January 6, 1982, to parents Michelle Rothschild-Patterson and Alex Pollard. Tiffany has a brother, Makkah Patterson.

She became interested in the world of acting in her childhood years. She is of American nationality. Furthermore, she belongs to an African-American ethnic background.

Talking about her education, Pollard attended John F. Kennedy Middle School. Later, she graduated from Thomas R. Proctor High School.

Tiffany Pollard: Career, Salary, Net Worth

Pollard initially appeared in the video for ‘Screech of the Decapitated’ in 2005. Additionally, she also played the role of Omunique’s Client in ‘First Sunday’ in 2008.

Similarly, in 2011, she appeared in ‘Drama: A Comedy Pilot’.  Since then, she has appeared in numerous other movies and television series. All in all, she has 8 credits as an actress.

Pollard has appeared in numerous TV series including ‘Scared Famous’, ‘Famously Single, ‘Brunch with Tiffany’, ‘Shane & Friends, ‘The Doctors’, ‘Big Brother’s Bit on the Side, ‘Up Late with Rylan’, ‘The Next 15’, ‘Celebrity Big Brother, ‘Reality Obsessed’, ‘New York Goes to Work’ and ‘Flavor of Love Girls: Charm School’ among others.

Furthermore, she also became part of the 2009 national tour of The Vagina Monologues which consisted of an all-black cast.

Pollard garnered the Teen Choice Award nomination in 2007. She received a nomination for her performance in ‘I Love New York’ in the category of Choice TV: Female Reality/Variety Star.

She began hosting Hot Haus, a queer sex reality competition on OutTV in 2022.

Tiffany Pollard has not revealed her current salary. However, she has an estimated net worth of around $500 thousand at present.
