Bow Wow's Daughter Shai Moss Looks Beautiful in White Dress and Cute Hair Buns

American rapper Bow Wow's daughter, Shai Moss, made her movie debut and celebrated it with a beautiful photoshoot on her Instagram page.

Bow Wow's nine-year-old daughter, Shai Moss, booked her grand debut in the entertainment industry after several acting classes and auditions.

She celebrated this achievement in a beautiful white dress and pretty hair buns in a professional photoshoot by Dorris Stone and shared the news with her fans on Instagram.

Bow Wow at the set of BET 106 and Park on June 11, 2014 in New York City. | Photo: Getty Images

Shai did not share any details of the exciting gig but was quick and excited to mention that she wrapped up one gig and booked another. Shad "Bow Wow" Moss and Joie Chavis, the young star's proud parents, were quick to congratulate their baby girl on her first acting gig.

Shai is following in her father Bow Wow's footsteps. The rapper also started in the entertainment industry at the young age of 12. He featured in various blockbuster movies like "Fast and Furious."

Shai already has many followers on Instagram and TikTok and is gradually growing her fanbase. She participates in various fun challenges on social media and continues to gain more followers.

Bow Wow was initially skeptical about revealing Shai's identity to the public when she was just born in 2011.

Her swelling fan base loves her spirit, fun challenges, and the chemistry between her and her father. Shai also features on her parents' social media pages.

Shai's followers have been extremely encouraging and find her a talented entertainer in the making. They have encouraged her to go into the entertainment industry and make waves like her father.

Shai has interacted with a few celebrities who enjoyed her lively personality. They believe she has what it takes to make it in the entertainment industry.

Bow Wow's affection for his daughter on her acting career is in sharp contrast to allegations by his baby mama's sister, Najah Chavis. Najah called Bow Wow a deadbeat dad because of the way he acted following Shai's birth.

Bow Wow was initially skeptical about revealing Shai's identity to the public when she was just born in 2011. However, he later admitted he was a first-time father, revealing that he was in a dark place after her birth.

Hence, he was ready to keep her out of social media and popularity for some time when she was just born. Bow Wow also said he wanted Shai to have the life he never had.

Shai is now grown and dubbed her father's twin and the New Lil Bow Wow. Bow Wow is ready to retire at 33 and shift his focus on his family.

He plans to train his baby girl and make her the next big thing on the television screens. Things are already going according to plan.
