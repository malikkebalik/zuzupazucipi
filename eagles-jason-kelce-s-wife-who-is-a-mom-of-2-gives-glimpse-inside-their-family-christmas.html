Eagles' Jason Kelce's Wife Who Is a Mom of 2 Gives Glimpse inside Their Family Christmas

Unlike his younger brother Travis Kelce, a bachelor, Jason Kelce is a doting family man. His wife recently shared the family’s Christmas card with their brood.

Philadelphia Eagles' center player, Jason Kelce, settled down in April 2018 when he married Kylie McDevitt at Center City. The couple tied the knot at the Logan Hotel, and Sarah Alderman of the Coatesville-based AGPcollective served as the official photographer for the nuptials.

The event was planned by Sarah Bree of the Philly-based All About Events. About 170 guests attended the wedding, including the couple’s Irish wolfhound, Winnie.

KNOWING MCDEVITT & THEIR KIDS

Kelce and McDevitt met on the Tinder dating app. McDevitt is an American hockey player and media personality. She was born in Narbeth, Pennsylvania, and attended Lower Merion High School in Ardmore in Pennsylvania.

She and Kelce are parents to two kids. The duo welcomed their first child, daughter Wyatt Elizabeth who is two years old, in 2019.

At the time of their announcement, they shared images of their newborn on social media, who weighed in at 8 pounds, 6 ounces at birth.

Jason Kelce #62 of the Philadelphia Eagles walks off the field after defeating the Washington Football Team at Lincoln Financial Field on December 21, 2021 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania | Photo: Getty Images

"Chunked up the Deuces on her way out," the football player wrote about his daughter in his post, which was accompanied by a photo of his child holding up a peace sign.

On the other hand, mom McDevitt wrote that bringing Wyatt into the world took “a whole lot of patience and then some Pitocin," a hormone used to induce contractions during birth.

CHRISTMAS CARD

Kelce, 34, and McDevitt became parents for the second time when they welcomed another daughter named Elliotte Ray in March 2021. They received congratulatory messages on social media, including one from the University of Cincinnati.

Recently, his beloved spouse shared a portrait of the family Christmas card on her Instagram, which featured the couple's bundles of joy. McDevitt captioned the post:

“Merry Christmas! Love, the Kelces.”

The image showed the husband and wife holding their daughters all smiles, with the girls donning coordinated red and black outfits.

One other snapshot displayed the NFL star pictured outside admiring his eldest child, who seemed happy enough to offer a wide smile for the camera. The last photo featured the youngest family member who looked adorable in her plaided dress.

REALIZING FATHERHOOD

Apart from being a proud family man, Kelce is a four-time Pro Bowler (2014, 2016, 2019, 2020) and a three-time first-team All-Pro (2017, 2019), respectively.

Interestingly, all he ever wanted to be in life was to be a dad. When he was only two years old, Kelce told a nurse that he wanted to be a father when he grew up.
