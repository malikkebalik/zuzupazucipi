NYC home that appeared in 'Uncut Gems' lists for $14.99M

Explore More

This stylish New York City listing sure is a piece of “reel” estate.

If this condo unit at Trump World Tower, at 845 United Nations Plaza in Turtle Bay, looks familiar, it might be because it has served as a location in films, like “Uncut Gems” starring Adam Sandler and Julia Fox. In that film, the Passover scenes were filmed inside, we hear. Even the hit Netflix show “Narcos” also once filmed here.

Now the high-floor home with sweeping East River and city skyline views — as well as vistas of the Atlantic Ocean — has hit the market for $14.99 million.

The 5,425-square-foot residence can have a flexible layout with up to five bedrooms. Though, as it stands, there’s a home theater that can remain instead of creating a fifth bedroom, and that’s where a buyer can sit back and watch what’s been filmed right inside. The unit also fits a large entertaining space with 76 feet of south-facing windows.

Design details include marble floors, lacquered rosewood walls, coffered ceilings, custom cabinets and a gas fireplace.

The condo was formerly owned by the late real estate lawyer and investor Warren Estis, who passed away in 2022. He bought it for $10 million in 2009.

This building has also been home to Jay-Z and Derek Jeter.

Amy Herman and Ryan Serhant, both of Serhant, have the listing.
