Backstage footage shows KSI embracing brother Deji at weigh-in before Tommy Fury fight

KSI was delighted when he spotted his brother Deji and embraced him after Friday's weigh-in.

The YouTuber-turned-boxer is set to face Tommy Fury in Manchester, on the same event as Logan Paul vs Dillon Danis, live on talkSPORT.

Both men hit the scales and successfully made weight for the contest earlier in the day.

Then, at their ceremonial weigh-in, they faced off for the final time in the Misfits Boxing cage.

After this, they headed backstage - separately, of course.

KSI was taken by surprise as his brother Deji was waiting for him.

He instantly looked overjoyed as he embraced his brother.

The pair have had a strained relationship at times and even publicly feuded at one point several years ago.

Now, they are on much better terms and regularly support one another at their fights.

KSI was ecstatic when Deji won for the first time by beating Fousey last year.

And he also attended when his brother was stopped by Floyd Mayweather.
