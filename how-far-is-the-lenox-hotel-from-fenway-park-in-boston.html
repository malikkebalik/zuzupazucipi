How Far is the Lenox Hotel from Fenway Park in Boston?

If you’re looking to catch a Red Sox game at Fenway Park during your stay at the Lenox Hotel in Boston, you’ll be happy to know the iconic ballpark is just a short walk away. The Lenox offers a fantastic location right in Back Bay near major attractions like Fenway.

If you’re short on time, here’s a quick answer: The Lenox Hotel is 0.4 miles or approximately a 10 minute walk from Fenway Park in Boston.

The Distance Between the Lenox Hotel and Fenway Park

Straight-Line Distance

The Lenox Hotel and Fenway Park are located in close proximity to each other, making it convenient for visitors staying at the hotel to attend events at the park. The straight-line distance between the two locations is approximately 0.6 miles or about 1 kilometer.

This means that you can easily walk or drive to Fenway Park from the Lenox Hotel in a matter of minutes.

Walking Distance and Route

If you prefer to walk, the distance between the Lenox Hotel and Fenway Park can be covered in around 15 minutes. The most direct route is to head southwest on Boylston Street, then take a left on Ipswich Street. Continue straight until you reach Fenway Park on your left.

Along the way, you will pass by various shops, restaurants, and landmarks, allowing you to soak up the vibrant atmosphere of Boston.

Driving or Riding Distance and Route

If you prefer to drive or take a ride-sharing service, the distance between the Lenox Hotel and Fenway Park can be covered in approximately 5 minutes, depending on traffic conditions. The most straightforward route is to head southwest on Boylston Street, then take a left on Ipswich Street.

Continue straight until you reach Boylston Street again, where Fenway Park will be on your left. It is important to note that parking near Fenway Park can be limited and expensive during events, so it might be more convenient to use a ride-sharing service or public transportation.

For more information on the distance between the Lenox Hotel and Fenway Park, you can visit the official websites of the hotel (www.lenoxhotel.com) and Fenway Park (www.mlb.com/redsox/ballpark).

Getting to Fenway Park from the Lenox Hotel

Walking to Fenway Park

If you’re staying at the Lenox Hotel and want to enjoy a leisurely stroll to Fenway Park, you’re in luck! The hotel is conveniently located just a short 15-minute walk away from the iconic ballpark. As you make your way through the bustling streets of Boston, you’ll pass by charming brownstones, quaint shops, and bustling restaurants.

Feel free to take your time and soak in the vibrant atmosphere of the city as you make your way to the game.

Taking a Rideshare Service

If walking isn’t your preferred mode of transportation, don’t worry – there are plenty of rideshare services available in Boston. Whether you choose Uber or Lyft, you can easily request a ride right from the front entrance of the Lenox Hotel.

Within minutes, a friendly driver will arrive to pick you up and whisk you away to Fenway Park. Not only is this option convenient, but it also allows you to relax and enjoy the ride without the hassle of finding parking.

Using Public Transportation

Boston’s public transportation system, known as the MBTA or “T,” is an excellent option for getting to Fenway Park from the Lenox Hotel. The nearest T station to the hotel is the Hynes Convention Center station, which is just a short walk away.

From there, you can take the Green Line train (B, C, or D branch) to the Fenway station, which is located right across from the ballpark.

The MBTA offers various fare options, including single-ride tickets and unlimited daily passes, making it easy to choose the option that best suits your needs. Additionally, using public transportation not only saves you the hassle of traffic and parking but also allows you to fully immerse yourself in the local Bostonian experience.

Tips for Making the Trip Between the Lenox Hotel and Fenway

Allow Extra Time on Game Days

Attending a game at Fenway Park is an exciting experience, but it also means dealing with increased traffic and crowds. To ensure you arrive on time, it’s essential to allow extra time for your journey. Consider checking the game schedule ahead of time and planning accordingly.

If possible, leave your hotel well in advance to avoid any last-minute stress. Remember, it’s better to be early and enjoy the pre-game atmosphere than to rush and potentially miss out on the excitement.

Consider Skipping Driving

Driving in a busy city like Boston can be challenging, especially on game days when parking can be scarce and expensive. Instead of dealing with the hassle of finding a parking spot near Fenway Park, consider alternative modes of transportation.

The Lenox Hotel is conveniently located near public transportation options, including the subway, which provides easy access to the ballpark. Taking public transportation not only saves you the stress of driving but also allows you to enjoy the city sights without worrying about parking fees.

Wear Comfortable Walking Shoes

While the Lenox Hotel and Fenway Park are not too far apart, it’s still a good idea to wear comfortable walking shoes for the journey. Walking from the hotel to the ballpark gives you the opportunity to explore the vibrant city of Boston and soak in its unique atmosphere.

Plus, walking is a great way to stay active and burn off some calories before indulging in your favorite ballpark treats. So, put on your most comfortable pair of shoes and get ready to enjoy a leisurely stroll through the city streets.

Things to See and Do Nearby

Shopping on Newbury Street

Located just a short distance from the Lenox Hotel, Newbury Street is a shopper’s paradise. This iconic street is lined with an array of high-end boutiques, trendy shops, and unique retailers. From designer clothing to handmade jewelry, you’ll find it all on Newbury Street.

Take your time browsing through the stores, and don’t forget to stop for a coffee or a delicious meal at one of the many charming cafes and restaurants along the way.

Visiting the Museum of Fine Arts

If you’re a fan of art and culture, a visit to the Museum of Fine Arts is a must. Situated just a few minutes away from the Lenox Hotel, this world-renowned museum houses an extensive collection of artwork from various periods and cultures.

Marvel at the stunning masterpieces by famous artists such as Van Gogh, Monet, and Rembrandt. Explore the diverse exhibitions and immerse yourself in the beauty and creativity of the art world.

Strolling through the Public Garden

The Public Garden is a tranquil oasis nestled in the heart of Boston. Just a short stroll from the Lenox Hotel, this picturesque park offers a serene escape from the bustling city streets. Take a leisurely walk around the park’s lush gardens, admire the vibrant flowers, and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere.

Don’t miss the iconic Swan Boats, a beloved attraction that has been delighting visitors for over a century. Whether you’re looking for a quiet moment of reflection or a scenic spot for a picnic, the Public Garden is the perfect place to relax and unwind.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the closest train station to Fenway Park?

The closest train station to Fenway Park is the Kenmore Station. It is located just a short walk away from the ballpark, making it a convenient option for those looking to catch a game or event at Fenway.

How long does it take to walk from the Lenox Hotel to Fenway?

The Lenox Hotel is located approximately 0.7 miles away from Fenway Park. The walk from the hotel to the ballpark takes about 15 minutes, depending on your walking speed. It’s a pleasant stroll through the beautiful streets of Boston, so you can enjoy the scenery on your way to the game.

What is the cheapest transportation option?

If you’re looking for the cheapest transportation option to Fenway Park, taking the subway is your best bet. The Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority (MBTA), also known as the “T”, offers affordable fares and has a station conveniently located near Fenway Park.

You can use the Green Line to get to Fenway, with the Kenmore Station being the closest stop. Taking the T not only saves you money, but it also helps you avoid the hassle of finding parking in the busy Fenway area.

For more information on the MBTA and its schedules, you can visit their official website www.mbta.com.
