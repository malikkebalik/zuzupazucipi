Wallington, NJ – The Montclair State University community is grieving the unexpected loss of one of its students, Abderrahmane “Abdu” Mefti, a freshman from Wallington, New Jersey. Abdu passed away on Tuesday, October 17, leaving a profound impact on his family, friends, and the entire university community.

It is to be claimed by netizens on social media, “Montclair student was attacked and thrown from the 6th floor of the school by a group of zionists all for defending Palestine. while the school is saying his death was not a result of any criminal actions.”

The university and authorities have clarified that Abdu’s passing was not the result of any criminal actions, and it is believed to be a tragic case of suicide. This heartbreaking incident has triggered an outpouring of support and sympathy from fellow students, faculty, and staff at Montclair State University.

Abdu was a 2023 graduate of Wallington High School and had just begun his journey at the university. Described as a young man with a compassionate spirit, an infectious smile, and an outgoing personality, Abdu was loved by all who knew him. He brought positivity and joy wherever he went, leaving a lasting impression on those he encountered.

In the wake of this tragedy, the university has emphasized the availability of on-campus resources to support grieving students. Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) is accessible 24/7, providing vital assistance to those in need. Additionally, students can reach out to the Dean of Students Office for support during this difficult time.

A GoFundMe page has been set up by Daniel Jeetan to help Abdu’s family during this challenging period. Abdu was a beloved son and sibling, and the loss has created a void that words cannot express. The community is rallying together to support the family and honor Abdu’s memory.

This unfortunate event comes shortly after a separate tragic incident at the university involving a fatal fall from a parking deck. The community continues to support one another, emphasizing the need for compassion and unity during these challenging times.

Abdu’s passing is a somber reminder of the importance of mental health support and community unity. The Montclair State University community is united in grief, offering condolences to Abdu’s family and friends, and holding onto the memories of the vibrant and compassionate person he was. Abdu will be dearly missed, and his memory will live on in the hearts of all who knew him.
