The Rise of Plastic Bertrand

In 1978, a Belgian singer named Plastic Bertrand exploded onto the music scene with a high-energy punk rock track called “Ca Plane Pour Moi.” The song, which translates to “This Is Going to Be Good for Me,” was a massive success in Europe and even crossed over to the United States, where it peaked at number 47 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. But what was the inspiration behind this catchy and eccentric tune?

The Lyrics of “Ca Plane Pour Moi”

At first listen, “Ca Plane Pour Moi” may seem like a nonsensical string of words, but upon closer examination, the lyrics reveal a unique story. The song is an ode to the joys of life and living in the moment, with Plastic Bertrand proudly proclaiming that he sings, dances, and drinks to excess whenever he wants. The lyrics are full of playful references to French and Belgian cultural icons, as well as nods to American pop culture like James Dean and Marilyn Monroe.

The Cultural Impact of “Ca Plane Pour Moi”

Aside from its infectious beat and quirky lyrics, “Ca Plane Pour Moi” has had a lasting impact on popular culture. The song has been covered by numerous artists in a variety of languages, and has been featured in movies and television shows ranging from National Lampoon’s European Vacation to Glee. In 2018, Rolling Stone ranked “Ca Plane Pour Moi” as one of the 100 Greatest Debut Singles of All Time, cementing its place in music history.

The Controversy Surrounding “Ca Plane Pour Moi”

Despite its success, “Ca Plane Pour Moi” has not been without its share of controversy over the years. Some have accused Plastic Bertrand of lip-syncing his live performances, a claim he has vehemently denied. In addition, there have been questions raised about who actually wrote the song and whether Plastic Bertrand was merely a frontman. Although Plastic Bertrand has maintained that he wrote the lyrics and melody himself, it has been rumored that the true songwriter was actually Lou Deprijck, a Belgian producer who worked with Plastic Bertrand on his debut album.

The Legacy of Plastic Bertrand and “Ca Plane Pour Moi”

Despite the controversies and questions surrounding its origins, “Ca Plane Pour Moi” remains a beloved and influential song that continues to inspire new generations of artists. Plastic Bertrand himself has continued to perform and record music over the years, further cementing his place in music history. The lyrics of “Ca Plane Pour Moi” may be light and carefree, but they tap into a universal sentiment that transcends language and culture – the joy of living life to the fullest.
