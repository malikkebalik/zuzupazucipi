What events led to Bob Marley’s passing?

Marley performed his final show in Pittsburgh in September 1980 before canceling the rest of his live shows and departing for Germany for an aggressive diet-based therapy under Josef Issels after collapsing while jogging in Central Park during his final tour. When the treatment didn’t work after eight months, Marley flew back to Jamaica. As his condition deteriorated during the flight, he was sent to the hospital when he arrived in Miami, where he passed away on May 11, 1981.

Where was the funeral for Marley?

On May 21, Bob received a state funeral in Jamaica, with Prime Minister Edward Seaga delivering the eulogy. He was laid to rest alongside his Gibson Les Paul guitar at a chapel near his birthplace.
