Is Tim Curry Still Alive 2022? The Truth About His Health and Career

Tim Curry is a legendary actor, singer, and voice artist who has starred in iconic roles such as Dr. Frank-N-Furter in The Rocky Horror Picture Show, Pennywise the Clown in It, and Lord of Darkness in Legend. He has also appeared in numerous stage productions, TV shows, and animated films. But what is the current status of his health and career? Is Tim Curry still alive 2022?

Tim Curry’s Stroke and Recovery

In July 2012, Tim Curry suffered a major stroke that left him unable to walk or stand. He has been using a wheelchair ever since. The stroke also affected his speech and mobility, but not his sense of humor or passion for acting. According to his agent, Marcia Hurwitz, he has been going to physical therapy and doing very well. He also thanks everyone for sending good wishes.

Tim Curry has been very selective about his public appearances after his stroke. He has mostly focused on voice acting, although he has occasionally performed as a singer and attended fan conventions. In 2015, he received a lifetime achievement award at the Tony Awards, where he expressed his gratitude for the recognition. He said: “It means that it just sort of solidifies the kind of work the American acting community has given me for years now. It’s very gracious of them, I think. I was thrilled when they told me and I am thrilled now.”

In October 2021, Tim Curry attended a Manchester event called For The Love of Horror 4, where he met fans and signed autographs. Photos from the event show that Tim Curry still uses a wheelchair and appears to be frail, but also happy and enthusiastic. He also gave a message to his fans, saying: “I do try to look on the bright side of life, because I think it’s dull and annoying to look on the dark side of life and you’re no fun to be with. And I think that’s a personal responsibility of everyone.”

Tim Curry’s Recent and Upcoming Projects

Despite his health challenges, Tim Curry has not retired from acting. He has been lending his voice to various animated films and shows, such as Dagon: Troll World Chronicles, Star Wars: The Clone Wars, and Scooby-Doo and Guess Who? He has also reprised his role as Dr. Frank-N-Furter in a 2016 live TV remake of The Rocky Horror Picture Show, where he played the narrator. He said: “It’s neither a blessing or a curse. I was lucky to get it.”

Tim Curry has also been involved in some political and charitable causes. In 2020, he participated in a fundraising event for the Democratic Party of Wisconsin, where he joined the original cast of The Rocky Horror Picture Show for a live stream. He also supported the Actors Fund, a nonprofit organization that helps entertainment professionals in need. He said: “I’m very happy to be here and help the Actors Fund. I think it’s a wonderful organization.”

As of 2022, Tim Curry has one upcoming project in the works. He is set to voice the role of Necrofer the Death Bringer in the animated film Dagon: Troll World Chronicles, which is expected to be released in 2022. The film is based on the fantasy novels by Brian Jacques and features a cast of anthropomorphic animals. Tim Curry will play the main antagonist, a tyrannical warlord who seeks to conquer the world.

Conclusion

Tim Curry is still alive 2022, but he has been living with the effects of a stroke for almost a decade. He has been using a wheelchair and limiting his public appearances, but he has not given up on his love for acting, singing, and entertaining. He has been working on voice roles and supporting various causes, while also appreciating his fans and his legacy. He is a true inspiration and a living legend.
