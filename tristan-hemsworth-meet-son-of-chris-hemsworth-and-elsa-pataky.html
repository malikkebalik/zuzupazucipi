Has Two Siblings- India and Sasha Hemsworth

Tristan Hemsworth, who is six years old, isn't the single kid of his mom and dad. He has a twin brother named Sasha Hemsworth and an elder sister, India Rose Hemsworth.

Tristan and Sasha were born on March 18, 2014, in Los Angeles. During their birth time, "You are home! Tristan and Sasha came to this world on [Tuesday,] March 18 just after [the] full moon,” the actress wrote on her Instagram.

On the other hand, India was born May 11, 2012, in London, United Kingdom. India's name originally comes from India, where Chris shoot for his Netflix film, Extraction.

India Rose is now eight years and is two years older than Tristan and Sasha. Currently, the siblings are still in their early days and their parents make an effort and try to stay together as much as they can. 

Parents' (Chris and Elsa) One Decade Of Married Life

Tristan's mom, Elsa and dad, Chris, have been enjoying their marital relationship for over one decade. In early 2020, the couple has celebrated the 10 years of a marriage anniversary.

The Ghostbusters actor and Snakes on a Plane actress first met in early 2010 through their mutual representatives, William Ward. Pataky told on Daily Telegraph’s Stellar magazine, "When she met Chris, he was just starting”.

Their whirlwind romance went public by the end of September. On December 26, 2010, the lovebirds exchanged the vows over Christmas holidays in Australia. "Getting married fastly just felt right," the Home and Away star stated.

Before nuptials, they have dated each other for less than a year.
