Zab Judah Bio (Wiki)

Who is Zab Judah?

Zab Judah is an American boxer who has held IBF, WBO junior welterweight, undisputed welterweight, IBF junior welterweight, and the lineal champion (2005-2006) titles.

How old is Zab Judah?

He was born on October 27, 1977, in Brownsville, Brooklyn, New York City, New York, USA. His birth name is  Zabdiel Judah and he is currently 44 years old. He was born to his father Yoel Judah, a former boxer, and mother Katherine Judah.

He has 11 siblings- nine brothers and two sisters. Five of his brothers are boxers, three of them are Joseph Judah, Daniel Judah, and Josiah Judah.

Zab Judah holds American citizenship but their ethnicity is unknown.

Zab Judah: Education, School/College University

Talking about Zab Judah’s education history, he has not disclosed the educational institution names he attended.

Zab Judah: Professional Life and Career

He made his career debut on September 20, 1996, in Miami, Florida. He defeated Michael Johnson by technical knockout in the second round and also defeated  Angel Beltre in the second round on April 14, 1998. His forehead was cut after he accidentally butted heads with Esteban Flores in a fight in Mach 1999.

Zab Judah made his first title defense against Junior Witter in Glasgow, Scotland on June 20, 2000, and defeated former IBF Light Welterweight Champion Terron Millett on August 5, 2000, at the Mohegan Sun in Connecticut.

In addition, Zab Judah defeated Allan Vester on June 23, 2001, by third-knock out in his fifth successful defense of the IBF title. He fought with Tszyu on November 3, 2001, at the MGM Grand in Las Vegas, Nevada for the Undisputed Light Welterweight Championship, and Judah entered the fight with a 3-to-1 win.

Similarly, he played in the welterweight in 2008, in light welterweight in 2010 and 2013, and then took a hiatus for over 3 years and returned in January 2017. Moreover, he has fought against Micky Ward, Wilfredo Negron, Matthysee, Amir Khan, etc.

Zab Judah: Awards, Nominations

He has won the titles such as Interim IBF Light Welterweight, USBA Light Welterweight, etc.

Zab Judah: Net Worth($8M), Income, Salary

As of 2023’s data, Zab Judah has an estimated net worth of around $8 million and he has earned that sum of money from his professional career. His career has helped him in earning both fame and fortune.

Zab Judah: Rumors and Controversy/Scandal

He was arrested on the charges of involvement in a fight at Stereo nightclub in New York in July 2006. In addition, he has also been charged with Domestic Violence in 2016 and jailed in 2017 for failing to pay child support.

How tall is Zab Judah?

Zab Judah has dark brown eyes and is bald-headed. He stands at a height of 5 ft. 8 inches and he weighs 64 kg.
