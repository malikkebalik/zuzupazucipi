Sylvester Stallone &amp; Jennifer Flavin Romance Timeline: Photos Hollywood Life

Sylvester Stallone’s third marriage almost ended, but is back on track. The actor wed model Jennifer Flavin in 1997 and after 25 years of marriage and in Aug. 2022, it looked like things were over after she filed for divorce in Palm Beach, Florida. Sylvester’s rep Michelle Bega gave HollywoodLife this statement from the Oscar nominee at the time, saying, “I love my family. We are amicably and privately addressing these personal issues.”

Things took a happier turn just weeks later. By the end of Sep. 2022, the duo (who did not have a pre-nup) called their divorce off, with an insider telling Page Six, they were “extremely happy.”

Jennifer is the mother of Sly’s three daughters Sistine, Sophia, and Scarlet. He was previously wed to Brigitte Nielsen (m. 1985–1987) and Sasha Czack (m. 1974–1985), the mother is his two older sons.

It seems like Slyvester and Jennifer’s best days aren’t behind them. Here, the former couple appears in a glam moment during the 72nd annual Cannes Film Festival in France on May 24, 2019. They were there for a screening of his film ‘Rambo V: Last Blood’.
