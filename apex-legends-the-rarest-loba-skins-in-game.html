Apex Legends: The Rarest Loba Skins in game

Because of her killer sense of fashion, Loba is the Apex Legends character you’d expect to have the best skins. It makes sense: the game’s lore revealed that she was born into a wealthy family before becoming an expert thief. It stands to reason that she would have the best equipment. Loba skins have clearly been a source of amusement for the developers since her debut in Season 5. She’s gotten a lot of wild and beautiful outfits from battle passes, events, and store drops. Several of these skins have become increasingly difficult to obtain over time, implying that anyone wearing one is a devoted Loba player.

When playing “Apex Legends,” players may wonder what the character’s rarest skin is and how to unlock it so they can brag about it to their allies and enemies. As for Loba, she is a Black-Market Boutique support Legend with access to 70 different skins. She has 19 Legendary, 13 Epic, 22 Rare, and 16 Common skins on her body. What, however, is the rarest of all of these?

Here are the rarest Loba skins in Apex Legends.

Game Master

In May 2020, Prime Gaming members received this rare-tier skin as a gift. While Rose Gold, a later Twitch Prime skin for Loba, was made available in December 2021, Game Master was released much earlier in the game’s life cycle and is therefore much more difficult to locate. There were a few extra steps needed to obtain this elegant brown and turquoise skin, as players had to be Prime Gaming subscribers in May of 2020 and manually activate the reward from their account.

Breaking the Law

With a short haircut and obvious tattoos, this Legendary skin completely changes Loba’s vibe. When The Legend loses her corset, she feels like a true outlaw who has compromised style for functionality. Since it was given as a reward for levelling up in Battle Pass season 12, which ran in the spring of 2022, this skin will become more and more scarce with each new season.

PS Plus skins

In previous seasons, PS Plus subscribers received the following skins. They are exclusive because they haven’t appeared since.

  • Ocean Groove (season 7)
  • Retro Wave (season 15)

Huntswoman

Huntswoman is now nearly impossible to find, much like Game Master. Players who completed level 53 of the season five battle pass—the same season that Loba was released—were given access to this rare-tier skin. Since battle pass skins, unlike event skins, are typically not reissued after their initial release, there is currently no way to obtain this Loba skin with an antique design.

Poison Dart

Players who completed the battle pass for season 11 at level one received a Poison Dart. Even though season 11 is still relatively recent, battle pass skins are some of the hardest to come by in the whole game. Similar to Hunstwoman, there is virtually no chance that this skin will ever reappear or be offered for sale, meaning that its scarcity will increase over time. Although it isn’t one of Loba’s more attractive skins, it is undoubtedly unique.
