Who Is Wynonna Judd? - Wynonna Judd Wikipedia

Wynonna Judd Wiki: - Wynonna Judd is a renowned American country music singer. She first became well-known in the 1980s as part of the mother-daughter country music group The Judds, with her mother Naomi. They had 26 singles released on Curb Records, including 14 No. 1 hits, and seven albums issued. Following the Judds' dissolution in 1991, Wynonna started a solo career.

In addition to eight studio albums, two compilation albums, a live album, and a Christmas album, Wynonna has released over 20 singles during her solo career. "She Is His Only Need," "I Saw the Light," and "No One Else on Earth," her first three singles, all peaked at No. 1 on the US Billboard Hot Country Songs list. Wynonna & the Big Noise, her most recent album, was published on February 12, 2016.

As a member of The Judds, Wynonna was inducted into the Country Music Hall of Fame in 2022. Her recognition and accolades are among the highest of any female country music artist in history.
