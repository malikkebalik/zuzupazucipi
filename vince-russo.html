Biography

Vince Russo was born in Farmingville, NY on January 24, 1961. Writer who is best known for his work with World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) and World Championship Wrestling (WCW). He worked with the companies as a chief writer, and contributed various storylines to each brand throughout the 1990s and 2000s. In 2000, he was involved in a dispute with Hulk Hogan over his character’s WCW storyline. He graduated from the University of Southern Indiana in 1983 with a journalism degree. He began his career in pro wrestling when he began training as a wrestler with Johnny Rodz in New York City.
