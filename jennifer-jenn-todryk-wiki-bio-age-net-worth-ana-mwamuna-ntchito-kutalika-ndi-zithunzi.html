Ntchito ya Jennifer Todryk | Mwachidule

Jennifer adayamba ntchito yake yamafashoni atamaliza maphunziro ake ndikugwira ntchito ku Zara ndi kosatha21. Asanakwatiwe, adakhala kwa Marie Claire kwakanthawi. Ntchito yake inayamba pamene adayambitsa blog yake ya moyo, Life as Rambling Redhead mu 2015. Pa njira iyi, amagawana nkhani zoseketsa ndikukamba za mafashoni, nyumba, kulera, ndi zokongoletsera. Jen adakhudzidwa kwambiri ndi zolemba zake zoseketsa zamabulogu komanso zomwe amakonda. Anayambanso kupanga otsatira. Kutchuka kwake kudakula pamasamba ochezera. Analembanso buku lotchedwa "W (h?)INE: Vinyo 50 omwe amayenda bwino ndi khalidwe lovunda la mwana wanu" mu 2017, zomwe zinali zoseketsa ponena za kulera ndi zovuta zake.

Analandira chidwi kwambiri ndipo amakondedwabe ndi owerenga. Mwamuna wake alinso ndi bar ya khofi ya Armor ndi malo ogwirira ntchito, komwe amayesa kupereka pang'ono kwa aliyense. Cafeyo idakhazikitsidwa ndi banjali mu 2019 ndipo ikugwirabe ntchito. Posachedwapa, adayambitsa kanema wawayilesi pomwe adawonekera pa kanema wa HGTV No Demo Reno. Kusintha kwa Jennifer kwa nyumba kukhala nyumba kunali kopambana kwambiri. Ziwonetserozi komanso kukonzanso nyumba zakhala zopambana kwambiri pantchito yake.

Jennifer Todryk Husband ndi ndani?

Jennifer anakwatiwa ndi Mike Todryk, Msilikali Wankhondo. Mike adapempha chibwenzi ndi Jennifer atakumana ku Zara. Ngakhale kuti sanafotokoze zambiri zokhudza moyo wawo wachinsinsi, n’zodziwikiratu kuti anali limodzi kwa kanthawi asanakwatirane ku Mexico ali aang’ono. Ali ndi ana atatu pamodzi, Vivianne ndi Ron. Ron, mwana wake wamkulu wazaka zisanu ndi ziwiri, ndiye womaliza. Vivianne ali ndi zaka ziwiri. Amakonda nyama ndi ziweto ndipo ali ndi galu woyera Retriever dzina lake Gary. Panopa amakhala ku Texas. Wopanga ndi woyimira bizinesi.
