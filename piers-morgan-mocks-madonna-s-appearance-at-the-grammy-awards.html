Piers Morgan mocks Madonna's appearance at the Grammy Awards

The appearance of Madonna at the 2023 Grammy Awards left many fans puzzled, with numerous individuals taking to social media to express their surprise at the 64-year-old songstress’s altered look. The singer appeared on stage to introduce Kim Petras and Sam Smith for their performance of “Unholy,” prompting fans to comment on her appearance, particularly focusing on her face. One fan tweeted, “Me attempting to ignore the fact that Madonna has a whole new face. #GRAMMYs,” alongside a meme video of Adele looking confused at a basketball game.

Another individual questioned, “Whaaaaaat happened to Madonna’s face?! #GRAMMYs,” while someone else remarked, “This legit looks like a Madonna impersonator with pics of the real Madonna in the background. She really f–ked up her face,” referencing photos projected on the stage of Madonna’s most iconic looks over the years.

Piers Morgan seized the opportunity to share his thoughts on Madonna’s appearance, taking to Instagram to post photos of the singer from the 2023 Grammy Awards with the caption, “I thought Halloween was in October?”
