Euphoria Season 2 Episode 3 recap: Cals childhood past is uncovered

The childhood and past of the Cal Jacobs (Eric Dane) is revealed that helps better inform this controversial character in the third episode of Euphoria Season 2.

The episode features flashbacks to Cal’s past, while, in present day, Jules (Hunter Vaughn) confronts Rue’s (Zendaya) new friend Elliot (Dominic Fike), who Rue has been doing drugs with since New Year’s Eve.

Cassie (Sydney Sweeney) also must come to grips with Nate (Jacob Elordi) ignoring her after they had sex, while Rue comes up with a ‘business opportunity.

The episode begins with Rue talking in voice over about Cal, who, when he was in high school, ‘spent every day with his best friend Derek.’

The young Cal is seen driving his Jeep and picking up his friend as they’re seen in wrestling practice.

They go get a milkshake after wrestling practice, with Rue adding in voice over that they could, ‘talk for hours’ or they could ‘just sit in silence.’

During his junior year of high school he met Marsha, whose seen teasing him with her feet while he drives his Jeep.

‘Marsha was way more forward than any girl he met before, and even though he was uncomfortable, he’s glad she pushed him… and he couldn’t wait to tell Derek,’ Rue said.

He tells Derek the story and when he says he came in her mouth, Derek said he was a keeper before Cal’s angry father comes into the room.

He tells Cal that his dinner is on the table in an awkward conversation before we see his family at dinner and he tells them he met a girl.

Derek met a girl too and they’re both seen making out with their girls before they all get naked and jump in the pool.

We see the first time Derek and his girl Marsha have sex, which ‘became a regular thing’  and she would tell him what he was good at.

He really liked going down on her but when he tells Derek he says he loves women but he’d never ‘eat p***y.’

Cal was heading to Duke and Derek was going to a ‘state school out west,’ as they’re seen celebrating their graduation.

Derek insisted they go celebrate just the two of them as they head to a bar where they know they’d get served.

They head inside – soaking wet from the rain – when they start doing shots, seemingly unaware that it’s a gay bar.

Derek puts a song on the jukebox as they start dancing in the bar and getting close when they share a kiss… and then another… and then another, each longer than the last.

The next morning Cal wakes up to his phone ringing and it’s his girlfriend Marsha, and he asks her what’s wrong.

He asks if the test is accurate and tells her to take another one but she said she took three, but it seems Cal is quite conflicted about this news that he’s about to have a kid.

He hangs up the phone and starts crying in his bed as the title card is shown.

The episode comes back to present day with a seemingly-high Rue dancing along to some song in her head… when she goes out to her kitchen and her sister Gia (Storm Reid) sees her and asks if she’s high.

She then tells the audience how to get away with being a drug addict, the first step of which is to get a ‘cover drug.’

She tells her sister that she’s gonna start smoking weed because it helps with her panic attacks but Gia freaks out and says she’s so selfish.

Rue says in voice over that Lexi is an ‘observer’ and everything in her life was a movie she’s writing, as we see Lexi taking over as creator/director of her own movie ‘This Is Life.’

Lexi and Rue are seen being ‘interviewed’ about her movie as we see Lexi starting to write her play as life unfolds around her.

Cassie wakes up at 4 AM to get ready for school and spent three hours getting ready, thinking about Nate, who completely ignores her in school.

She tries a number of different looks to try and get Nate’s attention, and one day he actually looks, which cheers her up immensely… until she runs into Maddy (Alexa Demie).

The vice principal says she can put the play on as we see Elliot and Rue and Jules all becoming friends.

Rue says in voice over that if she was a genius she’d figure out a way to do drugs for free as she comes up with a plan.

She tells Fezco (Angus Cloud) that she has a plan that starts with him fronting her like $5,000 worth of drugs.

She leaves and tells Elliot the plan and he says it’s a ‘good f***ing plan’ while Lexi starts promoting her play, but she didn’t tell Cassie about it.

Cassie and Maddy are at the house she babysits for as Cassie says Maddy just needs someone to ‘really truly love you.’

Maddy tells the boy that when he grows up he shouldn’t be an ‘a**hole to girls you like’ and he agrees.

Cassie and Nate continue their torrid affair behind Maddy’s back, while Cal and Cassie having sex while her parents are home.

‘What I would give to be back in high school again,’ Cal’s wife says as they listen to their son have sex.

Kat (Barbie Ferreira) has dinner with Ethan’s parents and she gives an awkward answer  which ruins the dinner.

Cal hasn’t been able to sleep since Nate told him about the recording that Maddy still has.

Rue gets dressed up and goes over to the Lori, the lady drug dealer from the premiere, to present her plan for selling drugs without getting caught.

She tells her it’s an amazing plan and she’s a genius and she fronts him a $50K suitcase, but she wants to go smaller so she goes with $10K.

Lori tells her that if she screws her she’ll sell her to some really terrible people and tells her she’s serious.

Ashtray starts beating him up with the butt of his shotgun and Ashtray tells him to call the cops but he won’t as Ash keeps beating him up.

Cal tells him that he had sex with Jules and he’s trying to get back the tape of them together but Fezco has no idea about any tape.

Fezco says that Nate is in love with Jules and Fezco and Cal are both very confused and Cal asks to just leave and promise he won’t say anything. 

Fezco agrees to let him go if he gets his son Nate to leave Rue and Jules alone for the end of time.

Elliot and Jules are talking at his place while Rue goes to a meeting with her suitcase full of drugs.

Elliot tells her she’s ‘very f***able’ and gives her tons of compliments before saying, ‘I’m sure Rue told you all that’ and said they’re in love.

Rue comes out of the meeting ass Ali asks what’s in the suitcase and she says school books but he clearly doesn’t believe her.

That sets him off and he says if she says one more disrespectful thing they’re done and she just says, ‘What are you gonna do, hit me?’

A distraught and confused Ali just walks back up the stairs as Rue drags her drug suitcase down the stairs.

Cassie goes to meet up with Nate but he texts her that something has come up… as he shows up at Maddy’s house with flowers, as the episode comes to a close.

Both Rue and Jules reunited briefly at the end of the second special episode, which were both set over Christmas, with this season premiere described as, ‘Rue and Jules cross paths for the first time since Christmas as East Highland rings in the new year.’

Rue had relapsed after Jules left her at the train station in the Season 1 finale, with Rue revealing to her addict friend Ali (Colman Domingo) during a Christmas Eve dinner that she didn’t know if she wanted to be clean… and that she didn’t even know if she wanted to be alive anymore.

While she seemed like she was in a better head space by the time Ali took her home, she got quite emotional while seeing Jules at the end of the second special episode.
